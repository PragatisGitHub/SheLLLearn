What is ShellLearn?

ShellLearn is an interactive learning tool that evaluates the writing skills of young children using a trained computer model.

Users can use the Panterro App to write letters and numbers, our model will evaluate their try and record meaningful data on their improvement.

There is positive reinforcement as well as encouragement to keep trying!

Who Are We?

ShellLearn was created by four students at Rutgers School of Continuing Studies as the final project for our Data Science and Visualization Boot Camp through Rutgers University.

Backend development was done mostly by Pragati Mehta and Saswati Mahap.

Frontend development was done mostly by Alfia Fahrutdinova and Jill Stratton.

However, each team member contributed in many ways to each part of the final project!
